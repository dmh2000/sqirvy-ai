About sqirvy.xyz

sqirvy.xyz is a web application that allows you to compare responses from different AI language models side by side.

Enter your prompt once and see how different models respond to the same input. This helps you understand the strengths and unique characteristics of each model.

Features:

  • Compare up to three different AI models simultaneously
  • Choose from multiple providers including Anthropic, Google, and OpenAI
  • Simple, clean interface focused on comparison
